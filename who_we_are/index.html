Who we are

Discover our common purpose that binds our diversity and culture together as a business entity and carve our authentic path towards becoming a Global Specialty Pharmaceutical company in the region.

Our Vision

As a Japan-based Global Specialty Pharmaceutical Company, we at Kyowa Kirin Asia Pacific are dedicated towards our patient health and wellbeing worldwide. We leverage on innovative drug discovery and global commercialization, driven by state of-the-art antibody technologies mainly in the core therapeutic areas of oncology, nephrology and immunology.
In Asia Pacific, we aim to be the best in class specialty pharmaceutical company in our therapeutic areas, and partner of choice for the commercialisation of specialty drugs.

Our Philosophy

Kyowa Kirin Asia Pacific aspire to collaborate with our counterparts to contribute to the health and well-being of people around the world by creating new value through the pursuits of advances in life sciences and technologies.

President message

Delivering life-changing value is at the heart of what we do.


Like you, every one of us at Kyowa Kirin is a parent, child, sibling, friend, relative. We know the pain of falling sick, and what it means to take care of loved ones who are ill. The knowledge that what we do brings relief to the lives of others, motivates us. Whether it is ensuring our products meet the highest standards of safety and quality, making them available in the pharmacy reliably, or disclosing unbiased information you need to make informed decisions to use them effectively, we are steadfast in our commitment to life.


As we enter a new decade forged in the wake of a global pandemic that has disrupted lives and accelerated changes to the way we live and work, we are determined to stay on the forefront of improving the way we deliver healthcare. Your suggestions, ideas, and thoughts are important to us. We encourage you to reach out to us whenever you have feedback that can help us deliver value better, if you wish to let us know how a patient is doing, or enquire about how you might.


We look forward to hearing from you,


Dr. Tan Boon Heon
