Life at KKAP

At KKAP, we believe that every patient is entitled to lead a quality life

Every job that we do is ingrained with this believe. As a team, we respect each member’s individuality and endeavour to maintain a friendly workplace environment.


In embracing innovation we have been able to develop a cutting edge pipeline of products to meet the needs of patients suffering from diseases for which no effective treatments are available. This approach makes a real difference to patients and their families, helping to bring both relief and hope.


To grow we need the best people. People who are passionate about making a real difference and never settle for the status quo. People with a passion for innovation and science

What openings are currently available?

In order for those of us at Kyowa Kirin Asia Pacific (KKAP) to meet the goals of our extraordinary mission to improve human health, we are continually looking for the very best and brightest to join in our efforts.
